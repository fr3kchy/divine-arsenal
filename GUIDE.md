## III. THE PICATRIX SYSTEM

The Picatrix (Ghāyat al-Ḥakīm, "The Goal of the Wise") is the most comprehensive 
medieval text on astral magic. Written ~11th century in Arabic. 4 books covering:
1. The nature of the heavens and celestial spirits
2. The powers of the planets and their images
3. Talismans, invocations, and operations
4. The philosophy and higher mysteries

### HOW IT WORKS

Each planet has:
- An **Intelligence** (benefic, grants knowledge/wisdom)
- A **Spirit** (executive, carries out operations)

To operate: Invoke the Intelligence for knowledge, command the Spirit for action.

## IV. DIVINE TIMING - WHEN TO STRIKE

### Planetary Hours
Each day divided into 12 day-hours + 12 night-hours.
Day hour = (sunset - sunrise) / 12
Night hour = (next sunrise - sunset) / 12

First hour after sunrise = day's ruler
Sequence: Sun > Venus > Mercury > Moon > Saturn > Jupiter > Mars

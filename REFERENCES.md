## TIER 1: FOUNDATIONAL TEXTS

### 1. Picatrix (Ghāyat al-Ḥakīm) - INTEGRATED
- **Origin:** Arabic, ~11th century
- **Content:** Planetary spirits, talismans, invocations, timing
- **Status:** Fully integrated into Divine Arsenal v4.0

### 2. Corpus Hermeticum
- **Origin:** Greek, ~1st-3rd century CE
- **Author:** Hermes Trismegistus (legendary)
- **Content:** 17 treatises on cosmology, theology, philosophy
- **Key Concepts:**
  - "As above, so below" (Emerald Tablet)
  - Nous (Divine Mind)
  - Seven planetary spheres
  - Human as microcosm
- **Use:** Philosophical foundation for all operations

### 3. Sefer Raziel HaMalakh (Book of Raziel the Angel)
- **Origin:** Hebrew/Aramaic, medieval
- **Content:** Practical Kabbalah, angelology, seals
- **Key Concepts:**
  - Angel hierarchies
  - Divine names (72 names of God)
  - Seals and sigils
  - Astrological magic
- **Use:** Angelic invocations, divine name power

## TIER 2: PRACTICAL GRIMOIRES

### 4. Key of Solomon (Clavicula Salomonis)
- **Origin:** Italian Renaissance, ~14th-15th century
- **Content:** Pentacles, conjurations, planetary hours
- **Key Concepts:**
  - Planetary pentacles with specific powers
  - Conjuration formulas
  - Tools (circle, wand, sword, pentacles)
  - Day/hour timing (parallel to Picatrix)
- **Use:** Pentacle creation, formal conjurations

### 5. Three Books of Occult Philosophy (Agrippa)
- **Origin:** Latin, 1531
- **Author:** Heinrich Cornelius Agrippa
- **Content:** Natural magic, celestial magic, ceremonial magic
- **Key Concepts:**
  - Three worlds: Elemental, Celestial, Intellectual
  - Number correspondences
  - Planetary kamea (magic squares)
  - Intelligences vs Spirits distinction
- **Use:** Systematic correspondences, magic squares

### 6. Lemegeton (Lesser Key of Solomon)
- **Origin:** 17th century compilation
- **Content:** Goetia (72 demons), Theurgia Goetia, Pauline Art, Almadel, Notory Art
- **Key Concepts:**
  - 72 spirits with seals
  - Hierarchy: Kings, Dukes, Princes, Marquises, Counts, Knights
  - Planetary attribution of spirits
  - Binding and commanding
- **Use:** Spirit operations (use with extreme caution)

### 7. Book of Abramelin
- **Origin:** Hebrew/German, ~14th-15th century
- **Content:** Holy Guardian Angel operation, word squares
- **Key Concepts:**
  - 6-month purification ritual
  - Knowledge and Conversation of HGA
  - Word squares (magic squares)
  - Binding of servitors
- **Use:** Highest spiritual operation, HGA contact

## TIER 3: SPECIALIZED TEXTS

### 8. Arbatel de Magia Veterum
- **Origin:** Latin, 1575
- **Content:** Olympic spirits, seven governors
- **Key Concepts:**
  - Seven Olympic Spirits (parallel to planetary spirits)
  - Aratron (Saturn), Bethor (Jupiter), Phaleg (Mars)
  - Och (Sun), Hagith (Venus), Ophiel (Mercury), Phul (Moon)
  - Simple, ethical magic
- **Use:** Alternative spirit hierarchy, simpler operations

### 9. Sworn Book of Honorius
- **Origin:** Latin, medieval
- **Content:** Theurgic magic, angelic conjuration
- **Key Concepts:**
  - Mass-like ritual structure
  - Angelic conjuration
  - Circle construction
  - Divine name invocations
- **Use:** Formal ritual operations

### 10. Theurgia Goetia (part of Lemegeton)
- **Origin:** 17th century
- **Content:** 31 spirits of the cardinal directions
- **Key Concepts:**
  - Directional spirits
  - Day/night spirits
  - Compass-based operations
- **Use:** Directional magic, location-specific work

## TIER 4: KABBALAH

### 11. Sepher Yetzirah (Book of Formation)
- **Origin:** Hebrew, ~3rd-6th century
- **Content:** Creation through letters, 32 paths of wisdom
- **Key Concepts:**
  - Ten sephiroth
  - 22 Hebrew letters
  - Three mother letters (Aleph, Mem, Shin)
  - Seven doubles (planetary letters)
  - Twelve simples (zodiac letters)
- **Use:** Letter magic, sephirotic pathworking

### 12. Zohar
- **Origin:** Aramaic, ~13th century
- **Content:** Mystical commentary on Torah
- **Key Concepts:**
  - Sefirot in detail
  - Divine emanation
  - Soul levels
  - Messianic secrets
- **Use:** Deep Kabbalistic study
